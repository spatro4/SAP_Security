## 30-Day Interview Sprint

For consultants with an interview scheduled within a month. This is triage, not mastery - it prioritizes breadth and pattern recognition over deep internals.

**Week 1 - Foundation Refresh**
Re-read ABAP Authorization, ECC Security, and Authorization Troubleshooting chapters. Drill SU53/ST01/STAUTHTRACE scenarios daily. Complete the Easy + Medium quiz sets for these categories.

**Week 2 - Cloud & Fiori**
Cover Fiori Security, Gateway Security, S/4HANA Security, and SAP BTP Security chapters. These dominate 2025-2026 interviews far more than classic ECC-only questions. Attempt 2 mock interview rounds focused on this scope.

**Week 3 - Identity & GRC**
Cover IAS, IPS, SSO/SAML/OAuth/Principal Propagation, and GRC/SoD/Audit chapters. Do the Scenario Labs for IAS login failures, IPS sync issues, and SAML troubleshooting - these are now asked in nearly every Architect-level round.

**Week 4 - Simulation & Gaps**
Run 3-4 full Mock Interview sessions at Architect/Manager difficulty across mixed categories. Review your Weak Areas widget on the dashboard daily and target flashcard review sessions specifically at flagged categories. Finish with a review of the Cheat Sheets and Architecture diagrams the night before.
