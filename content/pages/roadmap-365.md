## 365-Day Mastery Roadmap

For consultants building toward Principal Architect or Practice Lead over a full year, alongside active project work.

**Q1 - Rebuild the Fundamentals Properly**
Most 10+ year consultants have gaps in exactly the areas they stopped needing day-to-day. Spend Q1 on ABAP Authorization internals, SU24/SU25 mechanics, and classic ECC/network/transport security - the topics senior panels use to test whether "years of experience" translated into real depth.

**Q2 - Go All-In on Cloud**
SAP BTP Security, IAS, IPS, XSUAA, OAuth, Principal Propagation, and Cloud Connector. If your project background is ECC/on-prem heavy, this quarter is non-negotiable - 2025-2026 Architect interviews assume hybrid landscape fluency by default.

**Q3 - Governance, Audit, and Organizational Influence**
GRC Access Control internals, SoD risk rule design (not just running reports), audit framework fluency (SOX/ISO27001/NIST/CIS), and the Behavioral/Leadership/Stakeholder Management question sets. Principal Architect interviews test whether you can defend decisions to CISOs and audit committees, not just configure PFCG.

**Q4 - Real Implementation Reps**
Seek out (or volunteer for) at least one S/4HANA migration or greenfield role-redesign project this quarter. Pair it with the Production Support & Migration chapter and War-Room Troubleshooting exercises. Close the year with a full Principal Architect-difficulty mock interview series and a written case study of your own recent project, structured using the Root Cause Analysis exercise format.

Revisit this roadmap every 90 days - your Readiness Score and Weak Areas widget on the Dashboard should trend toward zero gaps by year end.
