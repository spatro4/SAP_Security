## 90-Day Architect Readiness Plan

For consultants targeting a genuine Architect or Lead promotion/interview, not just pattern-matching answers.

**Days 1-30 - Core Depth**
Complete every chapter's Beginner through Advanced sections across all 15 tracks in order on the Learning Path. Take the full Quiz Engine bank once per category at Easy/Medium difficulty. Keep a personal Notes entry per chapter capturing your own war-stories - interviewers can tell rehearsed answers from real experience.

**Days 31-60 - Architecture & Trade-offs**
Re-read every chapter's Architect Level Concepts, Architecture, and Decision Making sections. For each of the 12 Architecture Diagrams, redraw them from memory on a whiteboard or paper without looking, then compare. Complete all Hands-on Labs for tools you haven't used hands-on in 12+ months (SU24, STAUTHTRACE, NWA, STRUST, SICF).

**Days 61-75 - Scenario Immersion**
Work through all Scenario Labs, focusing especially on cross-system scenarios: Principal Propagation failures, Cloud Connector authorization, hybrid landscape troubleshooting. These are the questions that separate Senior Consultants from Architects - the panel wants to see you reason across system boundaries, not just within one transaction.

**Days 76-90 - Panel Simulation**
Run at least 6 Mock Interview sessions at Architect and Principal Architect difficulty, mixed categories, 90-120 seconds per question. Review the Audit & Compliance section thoroughly - Architect interviews increasingly probe audit and governance judgment, not just technical configuration. Finish with the 30-Day Sprint's Week 4 as a final week.
